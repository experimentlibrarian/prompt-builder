‚ö†Ô∏è Before you start: Using GenAI effectively for finding information

GenAI tools like Copilot Chat can be useful assistants, but only if you're in control:

  • ‚úÖ Know what tasks to delegate to AI
  • ‚úÖ Communicate what you want clearly
  • ‚úÖ Recognise when AI is helpful vs. when it's not

üö´ Why GenAI shouldn't find your sources

  • It can invent citations that don't exist
  • It can't access library databases where many of the best academic sources live
  • It often reflects dominant perspectives, potentially overlooking diverse viewpoints

‚úÖ Where GenAI can help

  • Identify keywords and related terms for your topic
  • Build effective search strings using Boolean operators
  • Get advice on refining your search results

üéØ Build your strong search prompt

A well-structured prompt may have four key elements: Goal, Context, Expectations, and Limits. Use the activities below to build prompts for different search tasks.

üìñ How these activities work

  1. Type your information in each of the four boxes (Goal, Context, Expectations, Limits)
  2. Watch your prompt build automatically in the green "Complete prompt" box
  3. Click the green box to copy your complete prompt
  4. Paste into Copilot Chat and check the response before moving to the next activity.

üîí Privacy reminder: Do not include personal information such as your name, student ID, or course codes in your prompts when using GenAI tools.

üìù Activity 1: Identify keywords and related terms

Use this prompt to help you identify the main concepts in your research topic and discover alternative keywords.

What do you want the GenAI tool to help you with?

üí° Try: "Identify the key concepts and suggest synonyms or related terms I can use to search academic databases"

What's your topic and background?

üí° Include: Your topic, your course/level, any specific focus areas

How do you want the response formatted?

üí° Specify: Format (list, table), number of items, level of detail

What constraints or boundaries do you want to set?

üí° Try: "Wait for my confirmation before proceeding" or "Ask me questions if anything is unclear"

üîß Activity 4: Get advice to refine your search

Getting too many, too few, or irrelevant results? Use this prompt to get advice on refining your search strategy.

üí° Try: "I'm getting too many/too few/irrelevant results. Suggest how to improve my search."

üí° Include: Your topic, current search string, what results you're getting, what's wrong with them

üí° Specify: Number of suggestions, level of explanation, focus on narrowing or broadening

üí° Try: "Let me try each suggestion before giving more" or "Focus only on search string changes"
